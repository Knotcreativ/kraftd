1. Introduction & Scope

Kraftd Inc. ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access and use our Platform and related services (collectively, "Service").

Scope: This Privacy Policy applies to all users, including visitors and registered users. By using Kraftd, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: First name, last name, email address, password, company name, industry
  • Profile Data: Job title, location, phone number, profile picture (optional)
  • Content & Documents: Documents, files, supplier data, pricing information, and communications you upload or enter
  • Communication: Emails, messages, support tickets, feedback, and survey responses
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method (processed securely; we do not store full card numbers)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, click patterns, search queries
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (we do not collect precise GPS location without consent)
  • Cookies & Identifiers: Unique identifiers to recognize you and remember preferences
  • Performance Metrics: Error logs, crash reports, and system performance data

2.3 Third-Party Sources

  • Authentication providers (Google, Microsoft) if you sign in with those services
  • Public supply chain databases (for enrichment and benchmarking)
  • Business information providers (to enhance insights)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide our Service
  • Consent: Where you have provided explicit, informed consent
  • Legitimate Interest: To operate, improve, and protect our Platform and service quality
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws (e.g., tax, fraud prevention)
  • Vital Interest: To protect security or prevent harm

We use collected data for the following purposes:

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your data in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We use third-party vendors to process data on our behalf (data processors):

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (Azure, AWS)
  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Analytics and monitoring services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Email and communication services

These vendors are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only as directed by us.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data when required by law, court order, subpoena, or government request. We will notify you unless legally prohibited.

5.3 Business Transactions

If Kraftd is acquired, merged, or its assets sold, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you and provide the opportunity to opt out if feasible.

5.4 Aggregated & De-Identified Data

We may share anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you without restriction.

5.5 With Your Consent

We may share data with third parties when you explicitly consent.

6. Data Security

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
  • Secure data centers with physical access controls
  • Employee training on data protection

No Guarantee: While we maintain reasonable security measures, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You use the Service at your own risk.

Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you without undue delay (within 72 hours where legally required) via email or prominent notice on the Platform.

7. Data Retention

Retention Periods: We retain your data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the Service
  • Fulfill the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy
  • Comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations
  • Establish, exercise, or defend legal claims

Account Deletion: Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Some data may be retained in backup systems for an additional 90 days.

Aggregated Data: Aggregated and de-identified data may be retained indefinitely.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

8.1 Right to Access

You have the right to obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

8.2 Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

8.3 Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

8.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

8.5 Right to Data Portability

You may request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

8.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing.

8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

Exercising Rights: To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kraftd.io with a clear description of your request. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law). We may require identity verification.

9. Cookies & Tracking

Cookie Policy: We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and Platform functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand usage patterns and improve service
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalize content and measure campaign effectiveness

Cookie Management: You can control cookies through browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality. Disabling analytics or marketing cookies will not impact service access.

Third-Party Analytics: We use services like Google Analytics. These providers set their own cookies and have their own privacy policies.

10. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before providing data.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete the data and terminate the account. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be effective upon posting. For material changes, we will provide notice via email or prominent Platform notification. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
