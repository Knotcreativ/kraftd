Product & Solution

What KRAFTD Does

KRAFTD automates the entire document intelligence workflow:

  • Document Ingestion: Upload PDFs, images, Word documents, Excel files, and text (up to 50 MB per file)
  • Intelligent Analysis: AI automatically identifies document type, extracts structured data, and maps relationships
  • Data Extraction: Extracts entities (names, dates, amounts, contact info), tables, form fields, and custom fields
  • Quality Assurance: Confidence scoring, anomaly detection, and human-in-the-loop validation
  • Export & Integration: Output as JSON, CSV, Excel, or API integration with business systems
