## 9. Common Questions (2 minutes)

### Q: How long does AI analysis take?
**A:** Usually 2-5 minutes for most contracts, up to 30 minutes for complex documents.

### Q: Can I upload while processing?
**A:** Yes! You can upload new documents while others are being analyzed.

### Q: What formats are supported?
**A:** PDF, DOCX (Word), TXT (Text). Max 10MB each.

### Q: Can I delete a document by mistake?
**A:** Yes, but you'll be asked to confirm. Keep backups of important files.

### Q: Who can see my documents?
**A:** Only you! Documents are encrypted and isolated by user.

### Q: How many documents can I upload?
**A:** Unlimited! Storage scales automatically.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes! Encrypted at rest, encrypted in transit (HTTPS), isolated by user.
