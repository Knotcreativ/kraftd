## RISK MITIGATION - SAUDI FOCUS

### Identified Risks & Solutions

**Risk 1: Government Procurement Cycles Long**
- **Risk:** 6-12 month approval cycles
- **Solution:** Start with private sector, build portfolio
- **Mitigation:** Parallel government + private sales

**Risk 2: Currency Risk (Saudi Riyal)**
- **Risk:** Exchange rate fluctuation
- **Solution:** Price in USD, accept SAR at rate
- **Mitigation:** Dollar-based contracts for enterprise

**Risk 3: Localization Costs High**
- **Risk:** Arabic UI, local compliance, local team
- **Solution:** Phased localization (start English)
- **Mitigation:** Allocate $100K Year 1 for localization

**Risk 4: Competition from Coupa/Ariba**
- **Risk:** Incumbents have resources
- **Solution:** Focus on SME/mid-market they ignore
- **Mitigation:** Build 50+ customer base before they notice

**Risk 5: Regulatory/Data Residency**
- **Risk:** Saudi data laws may require local hosting
- **Solution:** Deploy Saudi Azure region
- **Mitigation:** Already planned (Azure Saudi Arabia region)
